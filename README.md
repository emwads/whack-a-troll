## Gameplay
You and a second player have 30 seconds to smack down as many trolls as you can.
Just be sure not to smack the cute little moles while you're at it.

###Controls
Player 1:
Keys: 'w', 'a', 's', 'd'

Player 2:
Keys: 'p', 'l', ';', '''
