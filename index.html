Whack-a-Troll

Directions

Pesky Trolls have invaded your fields! Compete against another player to see how many you can smack away! However, be careful not to harm the cute little moles...

Player 1:

Use w, a, s, d keys to move your blue mallet and q or e to smack

Player 2:

Use p, l, ;, ' keys to move your blue mallet and o or ] to smack
